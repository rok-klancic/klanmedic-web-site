About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2010, LAEM has been at the forefront of paper product manufacturing, combining innovation with sustainability. What started as a small family business has grown into a trusted partner for businesses across the globe.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, sustainable paper products that meet the diverse needs of our customers while maintaining our commitment to environmental responsibility and manufacturing excellence.

Our Values

  • Quality: We never compromise on the quality of our products
  • Sustainability: Environmental responsibility is at the core of everything we do
  • Innovation: Continuously improving our products and processes
  • Customer Focus: Your success is our success

Manufacturing Capabilities

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility spans over 50,000 square feet, equipped with the latest technology and machinery. We have the capacity to produce millions of units monthly while maintaining strict quality control standards.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of over 200 professionals brings decades of combined experience in manufacturing, quality assurance, and customer service. We're committed to delivering excellence in every product we create.

Certifications & Quality Standards

Committed to the highest standards of quality and environmental responsibility

Quality Management System certification ensuring consistent quality in all our manufacturing processes.

FSC Certified
